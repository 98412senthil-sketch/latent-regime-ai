# Regime Decision Policy Layer

This document defines decision accountability over AI-discovered behavioral regimes.

| Regime | Business Meaning | Default Decision Intent | Human Owner |
|-------|-----------------|------------------------|------------|
| Dormant Risk | Inactive / churn risk customers | Reactivation & risk mitigation | Retail Ops Head |
| Normal Retail | Healthy retail customers | Retention & cross-sell | Branch Banking Head |
| High-Value Concentrated | Salary / corporate / affluent | Relationship management & wealth growth | Wealth / Priority Banking Head |

AI recommends regime membership.  
Humans own decisions triggered by these regimes.
